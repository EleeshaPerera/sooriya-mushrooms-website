Mushroom Nutrition

Mushrooms are a low-calorie, nutrient-rich food that provides essential vitamins and minerals for a healthy lifestyle.

  • Low calories
  • Natural plant-based protein
  • High dietary fiber
  • Vitamin B Complex
  • Vitamin D
  • Potassium, Iron and Copper

Health Benefits

  • Supports immune system
  • Rich in antioxidants
  • Helps maintain healthy bones
  • Supports brain health
  • Improves digestive health

Healthy Consumption Tips

  • Store mushrooms in refrigerator
  • Clean before cooking
  • Avoid overcooking to preserve nutrients
  • Add to soups, salads and curries
  • Use as a healthy meat alternative
