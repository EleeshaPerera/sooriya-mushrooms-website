About Us

Sooriya Mushrooms

Established in 2022 by Mrs. Anusha Tharangani, Sooriya Mushrooms was founded with a vision of producing fresh, healthy, and high-quality mushroom products while creating opportunities for women empowerment.

As a passionate entrepreneur, Mrs. Anusha Tharangani transformed her dedication into a women-led mushroom cultivation business. The company focuses on quality production, sustainable farming practices, and providing nutritious mushroom products to customers.

Sooriya Mushrooms continues to grow as a women empowerment initiative, supporting skill development and encouraging women's participation in agriculture and entrepreneurship.
